About Me

Hello! My name is Ben Bland and I am currently a student at Michigan State University. I am double majoring in English and Professional Writing. I am very passionate about books and hope to work in a publishing house working to print new books for generations to enjoy. When I'm not working with the written work, I enjoy spending time outdoors, playing my ukulele or nerding out over superheroes.
